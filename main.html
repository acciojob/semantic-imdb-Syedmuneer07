Tiger Zinda Hai

Actors Quote

Tiger Zinda Hai is an action thriller that follows Indian RAW agent Tiger and Pakistani ISI agent Zoya as they reunite to rescue hostages held by a terrorist group in Iraq. The film is inspired by real events and celebrates teamwork, bravery, and humanity beyond borders.

Actors In Movie

  • Salman Khan as Tiger (Avinash Singh Rathore) — RAW agent
  • Katrina Kaif as Zoya — ISI agent and Tiger’s partner
  • Paresh Rawal as Firdaus — undercover agent
  • Sajjad Delafrooz as Abu Usman — the antagonist

Favourite Quotes:

"Shikaar karne wale hamesha shikaar karne ka mauka dhoondte hain, par Tiger kabhi bhookha nahi rehta."

— Tiger, reminding us that true power lies in control and purpose.
